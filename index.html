Insist that your collision repair center use
Hyundai Genuine Parts.
If you are in an accident insist your repair center uses Hyundai Genuine Parts. Your repaired Hyundai’s appearance will not only be restored to its original condition, it’s overall structural integrity, integral safety systems and performance will also be maintained.
Go to HyundaiUSA.com/ConsumerAwareness/ to find out what to do in case of an accident.
If your Hyundai is ever in a collision, there’s only one way to be sure that it will be restored to its original, pre‑collision condition.
Often referred to as Original Equipment Manufacturer or “OEM” parts, Hyundai Genuine Parts are made with the same precise design specifications, exacting quality, and demanding performance standards as the parts that were originally used to build your Hyundai.
Using 100% Hyundai Genuine Parts ensures your Hyundai manufacturer’s warranties—and any applicable extended warranty—are intact and, in the process, helps to preserve your vehicle’s value.
